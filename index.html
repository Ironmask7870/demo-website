Learn a French word before you leave

Flâner

[flɑ.ne]

Click to translate

To wander, stroll aimlessly

A uniquely Parisian concept of walking around the streets of Paris without any specific destination, just enjoying the architecture and atmosphere.

"J'adore flâner dans les rues de Paris."
(I love to stroll aimlessly in the streets of Paris.)
